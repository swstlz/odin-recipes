Margherita Pizza

Description

This classic Margherita Pizza features a perfect blend of fresh basil, melted mozzarella cheese, and tangy tomato sauce atop a crispy, thin crust. Revered for its simplicity, this Neapolitan pizza pays homage to the colors of the Italian flag: green, white, and red. Baking at a high temperature ensures the crust chars just the right amount, akin to a traditional wood-fired pizza oven. Perfect for a quick dinner or a cozy gathering, this pizza promises a burst of flavors in every bite.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Tomato sauce
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to the highest setting, typically 500°F (260°C) or above.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the dough.
  4. Arrange slices of mozzarella cheese evenly on top of the sauce.
  5. Add fresh basil leaves and a drizzle of olive oil.
  6. Sprinkle a pinch of salt over the pizza.
  7. Bake in the oven for about 10-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbling.
  8. Remove from oven, let cool for a few minutes, then slice and serve.
