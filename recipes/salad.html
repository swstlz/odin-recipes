Chicken Caesar Salad

Description

This Chicken Caesar Salad recipe combines crisp romaine lettuce, tender grilled chicken, crunchy croutons, and creamy Caesar dressing for a hearty yet refreshing dish. It's a perfect option for a healthy lunch or a light dinner. The key to its distinctive flavor lies in the homemade Caesar dressing, which is rich with the tang of fresh lemon juice, the umami of anchovy paste, and a hint of garlic. Topped with freshly grated Parmesan cheese, this salad offers a satisfying blend of textures and flavors that make it a perennial favorite.

Ingredients

  • Romaine lettuce, washed and chopped
  • Grilled chicken breast, sliced
  • Croutons
  • Freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • Caesar dressing
  • Lemon wedges (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Wash and dry the romaine lettuce, then chop it into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Grill the chicken breast until fully cooked, let it rest for a few minutes, then slice it.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the romaine lettuce, sliced chicken, and croutons.
  4. Drizzle Caesar dressing over the salad and toss to coat everything evenly.
  5. Sprinkle freshly grated Parmesan cheese over the top.
  6. Serve the salad with lemon wedges on the side for additional flavoring.
